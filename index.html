FAQ

Common
Questions

Do you have a minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
No. We accept orders of any quantity — whether it's 1 piece or 1,000 pieces. There are absolutely no minimums. Every order, big or small, receives the same premium quality and care.
Can I fully customize my own design?
Yes. Every design can be fully customized — colors, logos, names, numbers, patterns, and graphics. With sublimation printing, the entire surface of the garment is your canvas. If you don't have a design ready, our team can help create one for you.
Do you deliver throughout Malaysia?
Yes. We deliver nationwide to all states in Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah, and Sarawak. All shipments are fully tracked and securely packaged to ensure your order arrives in perfect condition.
How do I get a quotation?
Simply contact us via WhatsApp and send your design requirements — the product type, quantity, and any design files or references. We'll respond with a transparent quote within 24 hours.
How long does production take?
Standard production takes 7–14 working days after design approval and payment. Rush orders may be available depending on our schedule — contact us to check availability.
What file format should my design be in?
We accept AI, CDR, PDF, PSD, and PNG files. Vector formats (AI or CDR) give the best print quality. If you only have a PNG or JPEG, ensure it's at least 300 DPI. We can also assist with design if needed.
